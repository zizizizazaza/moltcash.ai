## Design Context

### Users
- **Primary**: Startup founders seeking fundraising through tokenized revenue-based financing
- **Secondary**: Individual investors looking to invest in vetted startups via AIUSD stablecoin
- **Context**: Financial platform — users need to trust the system with real money and sensitive business data
- **Job to be done**: Founders verify their company, connect revenue, and raise capital. Investors discover, evaluate, and invest.

### Brand Personality
- **Professional** — Conveys financial credibility and institutional quality
- **Innovative** — Feels modern and forward-thinking (crypto/AI-native, not legacy fintech)
- **Trustworthy** — Every element reinforces reliability and security

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Visual tone**: Clean, refined, modern fintech with subtle warmth. Not cold/corporate, not flashy/crypto-bro.
- **References**: Manus.im (clean layout, purposeful whitespace), ChatGPT (refined minimalism, warm neutrals), Surf (polished modern feel)
- **Anti-references**: Generic crypto dashboards with neon gradients, overly playful/cartoon UIs, cluttered data-heavy screens
- **Theme**: Light mode primary. Dark accents for emphasis.
- **Color palette** (derived):
  - **Primary**: Indigo/deep blue — trust, professionalism (`oklch(55% 0.18 265)`)
  - **Secondary**: Teal/emerald — growth, success, money (`oklch(65% 0.15 165)`)
  - **Accent**: Warm amber — attention, action (`oklch(75% 0.15 75)`)
  - **Neutrals**: Warm-tinted grays (not pure gray — add subtle warm undertone)
  - **Avoid**: Purple-blue AI gradients, neon green crypto vibes, pure black/white

### Design Principles
1. **Clarity over cleverness** — Every element must communicate clearly. Financial data demands zero ambiguity.
2. **Earned color** — Color is used semantically (success, warning, category) not decoratively. Every color has a reason.
3. **Warm professionalism** — Balance institutional trust with human warmth. Avoid cold corporate sterility.
4. **Progressive disclosure** — Start simple, reveal complexity through interaction. Don't overwhelm on first impression.
5. **Purposeful whitespace** — Space is a design element, not emptiness. Let content breathe.
